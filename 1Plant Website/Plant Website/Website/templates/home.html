Who are we?

Here at Roots, located in the heart of Auckland, New Zealand, we pride ourselves on being more than just a plant and pot company, we're cultivators of dreams and nurturers of growth.

As a small business, quality assurance is at the forefront of everything we do. From the vibrant foliage of our plants to the artisanal craftsmanship of our pots, we hand-select each item with meticulous care, ensuring that only the finest specimens grace our shelves.

But our commitment to excellence doesn't stop there. Sustainability is woven into the fabric of our ethos, guiding every decision we make. From eco-friendly practices to community engagement, we strive to leave a positive impact on our environment and our community.

More than just a business, Roots is a gathering place for plant enthusiasts and dreamers alike. With our warm and welcoming storefront, we invite you to immerse yourself in a world of greenery and creativity. Whether you're attending one of our workshops or simply browsing our curated collection, you're sure to find inspiration and connection here.

As we continue to grow, we remain rooted in our values to quality, sustainability, and community. With every leaf that unfurls and every dream that takes root, Roots stands as a testament to the power of nature and the boundless potential of small businesses with big hearts.
